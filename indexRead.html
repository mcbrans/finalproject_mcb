  • A very important step in the combat against climate change was taken last year, under the flag of the UN, when 197 countries agreed to The Paris Agreement. The Paris Agreement its central aim is to strengthen the global response to the threat of climate change by keeping a global temperature rise this century well below 2 degrees Celsius above pre-industrial levels and to pursue efforts to limit the temperature increase even further to 1.5 degrees Celsius.
