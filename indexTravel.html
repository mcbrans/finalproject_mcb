Challenge 1

Travelling the world is bad for the planet. This year: skip the flight and go somewhere more nearby, preferably by train.

Challenge 2

When chosing a hotel this year: take an ecofriendly hotel, with respect for nature, its people and environment.

Challenge 3

Limit your water use, not only at home but also on vacation, especially in countries that have water storage.

Challenge 4

And if you really want to make an effort: do a staycation this year. Take your bike and go on a provincial road trip. You'll discover places you never were before, so closeby!
