@ Work

Challenge 1

Always saving lots of MB, also for the same documents? It takes a lot of energy to store those docs. Make arrangements with your colleagues what to store and by whom!

Challenge 2

Do you ever turn out the lights in your office? Start doing it now! And discuss with your office manager if it is possible to darken the communal areas earlier at night.

Challenge 3

Do you still print paper one sided? That is so middle ages! Start now with printing everything two sided, if at all. Of course it is better to not print at all!

Challenge 4

And do you use every piece of paper you print or have? Recycle your paper for a month. Making a grocery list - take an old envelop. and always also use the back side!
